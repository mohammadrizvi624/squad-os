|--------|---------|-----|------|-----------|---------|
| Daily standup | Daily | Mon–Fri | 9:00 am | Squad | Async/live sync on progress & blockers |
| Sprint planning | Bi-weekly | Thursday | 9:30 am | Squad | Plan the next sprint |
| Design sprint planning | Bi-weekly | Thursday | 3:00 pm | Design + squad | Plan design work for the sprint |
| Trio sync & planning | Weekly | Tuesday | 10:30 am | PM (Mohammad) + EM (Nev) + Designer (Sinnah) | Leadership alignment & planning |
