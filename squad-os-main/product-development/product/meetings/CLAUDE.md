# Meetings

Recurring meeting agendas, docs, and summaries for the squad.

Full schedule with times: [`cadences.md`](cadences.md).

## Recurring meetings

| Meeting | Folder | Cadence | Time (Toronto) | Attendees |
|---------|--------|---------|----------------|-----------|
| Daily standup | `standup/` | Daily | 9:00 am | Squad |
| Sprint planning | `sprint-planning/` | Bi-weekly (Thu) | 9:30 am | Squad |
| Design sprint planning | `design-sprint-planning/` | Bi-weekly (Thu) | 3:00 pm | Design + squad |
| Trio sync & planning | `trio-sync/` | Weekly (Tue) | 10:30 am | PM + EM + Designer |

> Business-stakeholder meetings (Product Ops, GTM, Marketing) are tracked
> elsewhere for now — add folders here if/when we want to capture them.

## Structure

Each meeting type gets a folder with up to:
- `docs/` — pre-meeting prep and agendas
- `summaries/` — post-meeting notes and action items

Folders are created when the first doc lands.

## Naming Convention

`docs/` and `summaries/` files: `YYYY-MM-DD.md` (or `YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md`).
