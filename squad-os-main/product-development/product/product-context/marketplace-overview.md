# Marketplace Experience — Product Overview

The product primer for the squad. If you're new here or starting an AI session,
read this first — it explains what we own, where it lives, and the language we use.

---

## What we own

The **Marketplace Experience** is a new product owned by the **Marketplace Experience squad**, a consumer-like shopping and discovery experience for B2B wholesale. It gives retailers a modern way to browse and shop with brands and to discover new brands — think of a consumer e-commerce shopping experience, but for B2B wholesale sourcing.

It launched **~August 2025 as a beta** to roughly **10,000 retailers** out of Lightspeed's total base of **~100,000 retailers**. It is currently still in beta, with a **General Access (GA) launch to the full retailer base targeted for August 17, 2026**.

The Marketplace Experience does **not** replace the legacy **Brand Portal** experience, which still exists on NuOrder. The two coexist.

## Where it lives: NuOrder

**NuOrder** is Lightspeed's B2B wholesale platform. It's where retailers (whom we call **buyers**) run their wholesale sourcing:

- Connect with brands they already work with and buy their products
- Browse brand catalogs
- Build line sheets
- Place and book orders
- Discover new brands they aren't yet connected to, view their catalogs, and make new connections — expanding their retail catalog, connections, and revenue

The Marketplace Experience is the new browse/shop/discover surface within this world; the Brand Portal is the established way of doing the same work.

## The two jobs we serve

1. **Source** — help buyers effortlessly source from brands they already trust.
2. **Discover** — help buyers seamlessly discover new brands they'll love, and turn those into active connections and orders.

## Where we sit in the org

Lightspeed → **Retail** org → **Wholesale B2B** → **Network Foundations** pod → **Marketplace Experience** squad (us).

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Buyer** | A retailer using NuOrder to source wholesale products. Our primary user. |
| **Brand** | A supplier/vendor whose products buyers source. The other side of the network. |
| **Network** | The connected graph of buyers and brands on the Wholesale platform. |
| **Connection** | An established relationship between a buyer and a brand that lets the buyer order from that brand. |
| **Connection request** | A buyer's request to connect with a brand (approved/declined by the brand). |
| **Connection activation** | A new connection becoming productive — the buyer places an order for an item from the newly connected brand (we measure this within 90 days). |
| **Catalog** | A brand's set of products available to buyers. |
| **Line sheet** | A curated list of products a buyer assembles when planning an order. |
| **Order / booking** | A buyer's wholesale purchase from a brand. |
| **Sourcing journey** | The buyer's path from searching/browsing to adding to cart and ordering. |
| **Discovery** | Finding brands a buyer isn't yet connected to. |
| **Brand Portal** | The legacy NuOrder experience for the same jobs; coexists with Marketplace. |
| **Marketplace Experience** | Our new consumer-like shopping & discovery experience (this product). |
