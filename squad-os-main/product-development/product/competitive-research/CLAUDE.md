# Competitive Research

Competitor intel for the Marketplace Experience and the broader B2B wholesale
sourcing/discovery space.

> **Status: not yet populated.** The squad wants to monitor competitors going
> forward. Add them here as we start tracking.

## Structure

| Path | What's here |
|------|-------------|
| `competitive-matrix.md` | At-a-glance comparison across all tracked competitors |
| `competitors/<name>/` | One folder per competitor: a `tldr.md` (what they are, strengths/weaknesses), plus notes, pricing, screenshots as useful |

## Adding a competitor

1. Create `competitors/<name>/tldr.md` — who they are, who they serve, what's strong/weak, why they matter to us.
2. Add a row to `competitive-matrix.md`.
3. Drop supporting artifacts (pricing, screenshots, notes) in the competitor's folder.

## What counts as a competitor here

B2B wholesale marketplaces / sourcing & brand-discovery platforms that overlap
with how buyers source from and discover brands — direct or adjacent. *(Define
the specific set with GTM/PMM — Kayla — when we start tracking.)*

## When to update

- A competitor launches or changes something material
- We hear a competitor mentioned in user interviews
- Ahead of GA / positioning conversations with GTM
- Periodic refresh of the matrix
