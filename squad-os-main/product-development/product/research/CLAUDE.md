# User & Customer Research

The squad's research layer — interview summaries and synthesized insights about
buyers (and brands) on the Marketplace Experience.

**Source of truth is [Dovetail](https://dovetail.com/).** This folder holds the
committed, linkable summaries and cross-cutting insights the squad references in
PRDs, strategy, and decisions — not raw recordings.

> We do **not** track named customer accounts here. There are no per-customer
> folders. This is about *what we're learning from users*, organized by study and
> theme — not a CRM.

## Structure

| Folder | What's here |
|--------|-------------|
| `interviews/` | One summary per interview or study — current solution, jobs, key quotes, takeaways. Use `_template-interview-summary.md` as the starting point. |
| `insights/` | Synthesized, cross-interview insights and themes — the "so what" that spans multiple conversations. |

## Adding research

1. Run/collect the interview in Dovetail as usual.
2. Write a summary into `interviews/` (copy the template). Include the Dovetail link.
3. When a pattern shows up across several interviews, capture it as an insight in `insights/` and link the supporting interviews.
