# Marketplace Experience — Vision

## North Star

> **Be the go-to place for network retailers to effortlessly source from brands they already trust and seamlessly discover new brands they'll love.**

## What this means

The Marketplace Experience wins when a buyer's *default* place to do wholesale sourcing on NuOrder is our experience — because it makes the routine job (reordering from trusted brands) effortless *and* makes the growth job (finding great new brands) something buyers actually enjoy and act on.

Two strategic thrusts follow directly from the North Star, and they map to our two objectives:

1. **Demand** — remove friction from the sourcing journey so buyers do more, faster, and come back. *(Effortlessly source from brands they trust.)*
2. **Density** — improve discovery and close the loop between buyers and new brands so the network grows and activates. *(Seamlessly discover new brands they'll love.)*

## Where we're headed

- **Now (beta):** proving the experience with ~10,000 retailers.
- **Aug 17, 2026 (GA):** the Marketplace Experience is available to the full ~100,000-retailer base as the modern way to source and discover on NuOrder, coexisting with the Brand Portal.
- **Beyond GA:** *(TBD — longer-term bets to be defined.)*
